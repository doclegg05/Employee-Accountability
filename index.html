Chapter 5

Victim vs Accountable Mindset

Two Roads

‚ùå Victim Road

  • Ignore the problem
  • Deny involvement
  • Blame someone else
  • Rationalize and justify
  • Resist getting involved
  • Hide to avoid it

‚úì Accountable Road

  • Recognize and own it
  • Forgive yourself and others
  • Self-examine contribution
  • Learn what to do differently
  • Act on solutions
  • Grow from experience

When facing a difficult situation, accountable people ask:

Not "how do I avoid it?"
but "how fast can I get through it?"

Accountable people face challenges head-on instead of running away.
